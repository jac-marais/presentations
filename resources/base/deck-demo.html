Slide Grouping

Navigation Groups

Slides declare their group via data attributes. The nav dots and badge react automatically.

<section class="slide"
  data-group="cerf"
  data-group-label="Vinton Cerf"
  data-group-color="#4ade80">
  <div class="kicker green">...</div>
  <h1>...</h1>
  <deck-diagram>...</deck-diagram>
</section>

Nav dots are tinted with the group color (dim when inactive, bright when active).

Badge appears top-left showing the group label. Hides when no label is set.

Tooltip on dot hover shows slide number and title, tinted in group color.
